CPU - The Brainpower Behind Your Beast

Every beast needs a brain, and in the world of PCs, that brain is the Central Processing Unit, or CPU. This crucial component is responsible for executing instructions and crunching data, making it the heartbeat of your computer.


Here at CheapBeast, we'll help you understand the ins and outs of CPUs, from core counts and clock speeds to compatibility with your motherboard. We'll also provide you with recommendations for powerful and affordable CPUs to make your build truly beastly!


Ready to unleash the full brainpower of your PC? Let's dive into the world of CPUs!


Getting Started Step-By-Step:

Step 1:

Research and select the right CPU for your needs. Consider factors like your budget, your usage (gaming, productivity, etc.), and compatibility with your chosen motherboard.

Step 2:

Prepare your workspace. Ensure it's clean, well-lit, and free of static electricity. Gather all necessary tools, such as a screwdriver and thermal paste.

Step 3:

Open the CPU socket on the motherboard by lifting the lever. Take note of the notches or markings on both the CPU and socket to align them correctly.

Step 4:

Gently lower the CPU into the socket, ensuring proper alignment. Avoid applying any pressure. Close the lever to secure the CPU in place.

Step 5:

Apply a pea-sized amount of thermal paste on the CPU and secure it with the cooler! But we'll talk more about cooling components later.
