Welcome to Motherboard City!

Ever heard of a spine? Well, your computer's got one too, and it's called the motherboard! It's the foundation that holds and connects all the other components in your beastly build. No pressure, right?

As the backbone of your PC, the motherboard is like a traffic cop, directing the flow of information between your computer's components. Make sure you choose the right one so your build doesn't end up with a bad case of digital scoliosis!

Here at CheapBeast, we'll help you navigate the motherboard jungle and find the perfect one for your beastly build. You'll learn about different types of motherboards, their features, compatibility, and more!

Ready to unleash the beast? Let's dive in!

Motherboard Installation:

Step 1:

 Choose the right motherboard for your system. Consider factors like compatibility with your CPU, the desired form factor, and desired features.

Step 2:

Install standoffs in your PC case, aligning them with the screw holes on your motherboard. This will provide support and prevent any direct contact between the motherboard and the case.

Step 3:

Carefully place the motherboard on the standoffs, ensuring the rear I/O panel lines up with the cutout on the case. Then, secure the motherboard with screws.

Step 4:

Connect the necessary cables, such as the 24-pin ATX power connector and 8-pin CPU power connector. Don't forget the case front panel connectors for power, reset, and LEDs.

Step 5:

Ensure all connections are secure and well-routed to prevent cable clutter or interference with other components.

Tip:

Install the CPU, RAM, and cooling solution on the motherboard before placing it in the case to make the installation process easier.
