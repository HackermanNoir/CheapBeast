Welcome to the World of RAM!

Think of RAM (Random Access Memory) like your PC's personal juggler. It keeps all the data your computer needs to access quickly up in the air, ready to catch and toss back when needed. With more RAM, your PC can juggle more stuff, like a multitasking maestro!

Here at CheapBeast, we'll help you choose the right amount and type of RAM for your beastly build, making sure your PC doesn't drop the ball!

Ready to turn your PC into a multitasking master? Let's get started!

RAM Installation:

Step 1:

Choose the suitable RAM for your system, factoring in capacity, speed, and compatibility with your motherboard. Remember, more memory equals more multitasking power!

Step 2:

Find the RAM slots on your motherboard and open the clips on both ends of the slots. They're just waiting to be filled!

Step 3:

Line up the notches on the RAM modules with the notches on the slots, and gently press down on the modules until they click into place. The clips should close automatically. It's like a high-five between your RAM and motherboard!

Step 4:

If you're installing multiple RAM modules, use the appropriate slots for dual or quad-channel configurations. Consult your motherboard's manual for the correct layout. Teamwork makes the dream work!

Step 5:

Once all modules are installed, double-check that they are fully seated and the clips are locked. Better safe than sorry!

Tip:

Avoid touching the gold contacts on the RAM modules, as the oils from your skin can cause corrosion or poor connections. Handle with care!
